Individual philanthropists can shape the future of our racing-driven impact. Donor-directed ideas and seed grants help us explore new programs that connect Montecito Racing with Cedars-Sinai cancer research and other aligned initiatives.

Donor Ideas & Seed Grants

As part of seed grants, a forum on the Montecito Racing portal will invite donor-directed concepts that teams and partners can apply for.

How donor-directed ideas work

  • Step 1: A philanthropist shares an idea or concept through the submission form.
  • Step 2: Montecito Racing reviews how it aligns with our endurance program and partner institutions like Cedars-Sinai.
  • Step 3: We follow up to refine scope, possible seed grant structure, and timing — informational only, no binding commitments.
